About Me —

Hi, My name is Hannah. I am a Graphic Design Student in Ottawa, Ontario. My skills include conceptual sketching, typography, and computer graphics. When I am not designing, I am honing my cooking skills, assembling Ikea furniture, and writing 'About Me' bios. On this page you will find multiple pieces from my first-year of Graphic Design at Algonquin College. I pride myself on the creativity of my ideas, my abilities to adhere to established timelines, and capability to work both in a team setting and independently.

For any general inquiries, please follow the "Contact Me" section for my socials and e-mail.
